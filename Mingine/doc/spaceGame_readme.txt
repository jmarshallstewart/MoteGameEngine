OVERVIEW:

Protect your cargo from aliens. Shoot the aliens that appear to earn a high score. The more cargo you have remaining, the greater multiplier you will receive from killing enemies.


HOW TO PLAY:

Use a game pad to play the game. Controls are mapped as per an XBOX 360 controller.

Game controls:
Start: Toggle Pause Screen
Left Stick: Move
Right Stick: Shoot
ESCAPE key: Quit to desktop
